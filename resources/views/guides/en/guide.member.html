Member Guide - MTAV

Welcome to MTAV, your housing cooperative's assignment system. This guide will help you understand how to use the application to participate in the fair housing assignment process.

What is MTAV?

MTAV (Best Technology for Housing Assignment) is an application designed to help housing cooperatives distribute units fairly among member families. The goal is for each family to receive housing as close as possible to what they would have chosen if they could choose freely.

Important Concepts

Family and Family Atomicity

Your family is the basic unit in MTAV. This means that:

  • All members of your family will be in the same project
  • Your family will receive a single housing unit
  • You cannot change projects independently of your family
  • Decisions about housing preferences are family-based

Unit Types

Your family is assigned to a specific unit type (e.g., "Large Family Home" or "Couple Apartment"). You can only choose from units of that type during the preference process.

Navigating the Application

Main Menu

In the left sidebar you'll find the main sections:

  • Dashboard: Summary of your project and current status
  • Gallery: Photos and videos of the project and community
  • Members: View other members and families in the project

User Menu

Click on your user information at the bottom of the left sidebar to access:

  • Settings: Update your personal information, change language, dark/light theme
  • Log Out: Exit the application

Quick Actions

In the top right corner you'll find the "Quick Actions" button for:

  • Inviting family members
  • Uploading multimedia to the project
  • Other actions based on your permissions

Managing Your Profile

Accessing Your Profile

To update your personal information:

  1. Click on your user information at the bottom of the left sidebar
  2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  3. This will take you to the settings page where you can edit your profile

Personal Information

You can update:

  • First and last name
  • Profile picture
  • Phone number
  • Password

Important note: You cannot change your email address for security reasons. If you need to change it, contact an administrator.

Managing Housing Preferences

How Do Preferences Work?

Preferences are your way of communicating to the system which housing units you prefer. You can rank up to 5 units in order of preference:

  1. First choice: Your most desired housing unit
  2. Second choice: Your second alternative
  3. And so on...

Setting Your Preferences

  1. Go to the Preferences section in the menu
  2. Review the available units of your assigned type
  3. Select the units you prefer
  4. Order them according to your preference (drag and drop)
  5. Save your changes

Modifying Preferences

Before the lottery: You can change your preferences freely as many times as you want.

After the lottery: Preferences are frozen permanently as an audit record.

Unit Information

For each unit you can see:

  • Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  • Square meters
  • Special characteristics (garden, balcony, etc.)
  • Location on the project blueprint (if available)

The Lottery Process

How Does It Work?

The lottery uses advanced mathematical methods to:

  • Consider ALL families' preferences simultaneously
  • Maximize overall community satisfaction
  • Assign housing units in the fairest possible way

It's not a random lottery - it's an intelligent calculation that balances everyone's desires.

Before the Lottery

  • ALL families must complete their preferences
  • The lottery cannot be executed if any family hasn't participated
  • You'll receive notifications if preferences are still missing

After the Lottery

  • You'll receive an email notification with the results
  • You can view your assigned housing unit in the application
  • Results are final and immutable
  • You can view your satisfaction score

Event Participation

Types of Events

Community Events: Meetings, social activities, assemblies

Lottery Events: The main event where housing assignment takes place

RSVP to Events

  1. Go to the Events section
  2. Click on the event you're interested in
  3. Select your response: "Will attend", "Will not attend", or "Maybe"
  4. Indicate how many family members will attend
  5. Save your response

Gallery and Media

Uploading Content

You can upload photos and videos related to:

  • Construction progress
  • Community events
  • Cooperative activities

Guidelines

  • Only upload appropriate content related to the project
  • Respect other members' privacy
  • Don't upload offensive or inappropriate content

Inviting Family Members

Who Can Invite?

Any member of a family can invite other relatives to join the project.

Invitation Process

  1. Go to your profile or the Families section
  2. Click "Invite Family Member"
  3. Enter the relative's email address
  4. The person will receive an invitation email
  5. They will automatically join your family and project

Important Information

Confidentiality

  • Your preferences are private until after the lottery
  • You cannot see other families' preferences
  • After the lottery, only final results are shown

Transparency

  • You can see all families and members in the project
  • You can see all available units
  • The lottery process is transparent and auditable

Support

If you need help:

  • Review this guide and the frequently asked questions
  • Contact an administrator through the application
  • Attend community meetings to clarify doubts

Complete Process: From Registration to Assignment

  1. Registration: You receive invitation and complete your profile
  2. Exploration: You learn about your project, units, and community
  3. Preferences: You select and order your preferred housing units
  4. Waiting: All families complete their preferences
  5. Lottery: The fair assignment calculation is executed
  6. Results: You receive your assigned housing unit
  7. Construction: You follow progress until delivery
